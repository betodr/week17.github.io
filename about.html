About Me

Alberto Diazsobhani

I am Alberto, a UX/UI Designer residing in Georgetown, Texas. I have the enthusiasm and hunger to create things that are beautiful and functional. I have empathy for others which propels my passion for creating useful and meaningful products. I am a creative, big-picture thinker, obsessed with crafting details. I am personable and energetic. I am ambitious, delivery focused and original. I enjoy collaborating with others. I am strong at communicating my intent, verbally and visually. I live to learn. I originally obtained a Bachelor’s Degree in Corporate Law from Universidad Autónoma de Coahuila in Mexico. Years later I discovered the fun in working with art and technology. I acquired a Certificate at the end of 2019 from the UX/UI Boot Camp at the University of Texas at Austin. UX/UI Design has opened my horizons to possibilities unknown. I aspire to simplify the relationship between people and the products. I utilize technologies to improve people’s lives in innovative ways. I design elegant solutions for my clients that are user-centered. I offer products via websites and mobile applications while still considering their goals and technological constraints. My goal is to create seamless experiences for all.

Oak & Thread.

Oaks & Threads furniture is a feature of Target, this subset for the brand has being integrated but keep its own looks and feels. Straightforward and easy checkout process, the inspiration from target itself and their furniture line, the style guide inspired in the users a homey feeling while they navigate through, the color palette evokes to be in a place with a classy selection of accents, the feedback from the stakeholder was positive and created on them the decided effect for what we wanted to inspired on the users.

 

Research

The research tell us that 46% of the participants are more likely to buy furniture online and the another 54% are likely to visit the sites only tio see products but make their purchase at a physical store. Creating a website where the user feels comfortable to shop around while also accommodating their needs to purchase our products.

The 66% of the users are around 35 & 55 years old, this is the main users to keep them in our site we styled a homey & classy look with the purpose to create in the users the feel of trustful brand that inspires the people create a new home atmosphere with our products
